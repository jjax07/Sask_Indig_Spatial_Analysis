## Verbatim Surrender Notes

> Initial survey in 1876, subdivision of farmland survey in 1889. Pressured surrender for sale of 16,077 acres as pursued by local settlers in 1906.

**Pasqua** (UNIQUE_ID: PRQA79-1, surrendered: 1906, dist: 9,402 m)
> Surrendered for sale in 1906, 16077 acres
> Initial survey in 1876, subdivision of farmland survey in 1889. Pressured surrender for sale of 16,077 acres as pursued by local settlers in 1906.

**Standing Buffalo** (UNIQUE_ID: PRQA78, surrendered: 1925, dist: 11,018 m)
> Purchased by Dept. 1925, Seemingly returned before 1972.
> Did not sign Treaty 4 but are protected under the Indian Act. Initial survey in 1881. Additions made of up to 560 acres in 1902/3. SE 6 22 14 W2 purchased by "Dept." 144 acres, seemingly returned prior to 1972 schedule.

**Muscowpetung** (UNIQUE_ID: PRQA80, surrendered: 1909, dist: 21,276 m)
> Surrendered for sale
> Initial survey 1881. Southern portion swapped in resurvey 1881. Suggestion of 17600 acres surrendered in 1909 report. 1913 schedule lists 17957.2 (including "For Balgonie Road 23.4 acres").

**Little Black Bear** (UNIQUE_ID: PRQA84, surrendered: 1928, dist: 34,353 m)
> Illegal surrender of 12461 acres in 1928.
> initial survey conducted in late 1880, no map until 1885. Illegal surrender in 1928 of 12461 acres.
