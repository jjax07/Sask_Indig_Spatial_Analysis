## Verbatim Surrender Notes

**Kahkewistahaw** (UNIQUE_ID: PRQA72, surrendered: 1907, dist: 1,240 m)
> Partially surrendered to be sold to settlers
> Settlers petitioned for surrender of reserve lands from 1885. 33281 acres surrendered in 1907.

**Ochapowace** (UNIQUE_ID: PRQA71, surrendered: 1919, dist: 5,019 m)
> Sold to Soldier Settlement Board in 1919, part of the soldier settlement act.
> Inital reserve alloted on north side of river in 1877, before being moved to the south side. Reserve surveyed in 1880. Refused Broadview petitioned surrender in 1907. Soldier Settlement Board surrender in 1919.

**Cowessess** (UNIQUE_ID: PRQA73, surrendered: 1907, dist: 7,475 m)
> SW section surrendered to be sold to settlers. North section surrendered for Cowessess boarding school.
> O'Soup's and followers initially settled on N side of Qu'appelle River, moved to the south side, provided 63 sq. mi., expanded to 78 sq. mi. in 1883. Settlers petitioned for surrender. 41% of land surrendered in 1907. 1908 school area surrendered.

**Fishing Station** (UNIQUE_ID: PRQA72A-1, surrendered: 1889, dist: 23,032 m)
> Southern portion was patented to the HBC prior to acceptance of the reserve in 1889. The 96 acres initially surveyed were reduced to 68.5.
> provided access to Crooked Lake for fishing. Surrendered in totatily sometime after 1913.

**Fishing Station** (UNIQUE_ID: PRQA72A-2, surrendered: 1924, dist: 23,796 m)
> Road Allowance transfered to government without compensation.
> provided access to Crooked Lake for fishing. Surrendered in totatily sometime after 1913.
