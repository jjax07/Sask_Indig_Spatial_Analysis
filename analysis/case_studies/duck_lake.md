## Verbatim Surrender Notes

**One Arrow** (UNIQUE_ID: PRF95, surrendered: 1899, dist: 10,726 m)
> Road Allowance surrender.
> Initially surveyed in 1881, overlapped St. Laurent Metis Settlement surveyed in 1878. Recognized in 1884 and resurveyed in 1888. Order in Council confirmed reserve in 1889. Surrender taken in 1895 for a road allowance.

**Stoney Knoll** (UNIQUE_ID: PRF107, surrendered: 1897, dist: 23,556 m)
> No uptake of reserve, returned to DIA in 1897 without acquiring surrender.
> Signed Treaty 6, 1876. Reserve surveyed 1879. No significant uptake according to DIA. 1897, reserve land "restored" to the departmnet. Deeded out to settlers. Marked as abandoned in 1877. No proper surrender acquisition.

**Chakastaypasin** (UNIQUE_ID: PRF98, surrendered: 1897, dist: 34,755 m)
> Illegal transfer of members and illegal total surrender as identified in ICC 2005
> Signed Treaty 6, 1876. Partially surveyed 1878. Following 1885 Resistance, many members moved to other reserves. Pressure increased to open up reserve land for settler use. Members transferred to Cumberland No. 100A, total surrender by OIC in 1897.

**Muskeg Lake** (UNIQUE_ID: PRF102, surrendered: 1919, dist: 40,161 m)
> Surrendered for sale to Soldier Resettlement Board
> Signed Treaty 6, 1876. Unclear on date of "uptake" of reserve. Initially surveyed in 1881. Subdivision survey in 1892. 8960 acres surrendered for sale to soldier resettlement board in 1919. Unsurrendered portion subdivided 1922.

**Mistawasis** (UNIQUE_ID: PRF103-2, surrendered: 1911, dist: 40,293 m)
> Surrendered for fencing materials and farming machinery
> Signed Treaty 6, 1876. Subdivision survery as part of severalty policy in 1891. Right of way surrender of 14.14 acres for CNR. 1607 acres surrendered in 1911, another 5028 acres in 1917, and 11520 in 1919 for soldier settlement.
